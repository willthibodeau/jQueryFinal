FAQ

What are fish?

Fish are cold-blooded, aquatic vertebrates that have gills, commonly have fins, and typically have an elongated body covered with scales. They live in the water.

What is aquaphobia?

Aquaphobia is the abnormal fear of the water.

What are aquaphobic fish?

Aquaphobic fish are fish with aquaphobia. They have an abnormal fear of the water, which is a bummer considering they are fish.

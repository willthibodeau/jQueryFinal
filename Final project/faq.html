Frequently Asked Quacks Questions

What is the most popular song about rubber ducks?

The most popular rubber duck song is Rubber Duckie, written by Jeff Moss and performed by Ernie on The Muppets. In September of 1970, it hit #16 on the Billboard Hot 100. In December of 1970, it hit #14 on the Australian Singles Chart.

Who has the most rubber ducks?

Hard to say. But in 2007, Guinness World Records awarded Charlotte Lee the honor of being named the owner of the largest rubber duck collection with 2,583 unique rubber ducks.

I don't know if that's sad or impressive.

Are all rubber ducks yellow?

No. Yellow is the most popular color for rubber ducks, but they have been manufactured in all colors.
